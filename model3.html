Joint Model: Conditional Generation with Instrumental Tokens

This model was trained using the chunked dataset with both vocal and instrumental tracks. It conditions on unique instrumental tokens to guide the generation of specific track classes, such as hi-hat, kick, bass, and full instrumental. By conditioning on these tokens, the model learns to generate all instrumental tracks in a single unified model, rather than training individual models for each track class.

Similar to the previous models, this approach uses positional embeddings to align temporal information, but it adds an additional layer of conditioning through the instrument tokens, enabling the model to generate specific tracks with better structure and coherence across the entire instrumental set.
